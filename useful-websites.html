Useful Websites You Didn't Know Exist

Hidden websites that can save time and boost productivity

The internet is full of amazing tools that most people never discover. These websites can help you edit PDFs, remove backgrounds from images, learn new skills, improve productivity, and save valuable time.

1. Remove.bg

Instantly remove image backgrounds using AI. Perfect for profile pictures, presentations, and graphic design work.

2. TinyWow

A collection of free PDF tools, image converters, AI writing tools, and video utilities.

3. Photopea

A free online alternative to Photoshop that runs directly in your browser.

4. Canva

Create social media posts, presentations, resumes, posters, and professional graphics easily.

5. Notion

Organize notes, projects, tasks, and personal knowledge in one place.

6. PDF24 Tools

Merge, split, compress, and convert PDF files completely free.

7. Archive.org

Access old websites, books, software, and digital archives.

8. 12ft.io

Helpful for reading articles that are hidden behind certain paywalls.

9. Google Fonts

Thousands of free fonts for websites, presentations, and design projects.

10. Canva Color Palette Generator

Generate beautiful color palettes from any image for branding and design.

Final Thoughts

These websites can significantly improve productivity and help you complete tasks faster without paying for expensive software. Bookmark them and start exploring today.
